# IAU Symposium 377 @ Malaysia

**Early Disk-Galaxy Formation from JWST to the Milky Way**

This repository contains the website for IAU Symposium 377, held in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia from February 6-10, 2023, with a Monsoon School from January 31 - February 4, 2023.

## Event Details

- **Conference**: February 6-10, 2023
- **Monsoon School**: January 31 - February 4, 2023
- **Location**: Pullman Kuala Lumpur Bangsar & Dewan Negara, Malaysia
- **Format**: Hybrid (In-person and Virtual)

The first-ever IAU Symposium in Malaysia and the largest professional astronomy conference in Malaysia to date.

## Scientific Focus

Connecting near-field cosmology with high-redshift observations, focusing on:
- First-year JWST and ALMA data
- The Local Group in a cosmological context
- Formation and evolution of disk galaxies at high redshifts
- Galactic archaeology and the early Milky Way
- Globular clusters in early structure formation
- Metal-free and nearly metal-free stars
